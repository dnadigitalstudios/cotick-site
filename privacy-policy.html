Privacy Policy — CoTick

Last updated: October 2025

CoTick ("we", "our", or "us") provides a simple shared task manager for families and small teams. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: phone number (required) and optional email address for account creation and login.
  • Profile data: display name and avatar initials used inside the app.
  • Usage data: task lists, team membership and app usage metrics used to provide the service and troubleshoot issues.
  • No financial data: CoTick does not collect payment or billing information.
  • No precise location: we do not access GPS/location unless you explicitly add location data to a task (and you will be prompted).

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and authenticate accounts and deliver core app features (teams, tasks, assignments, and synchronization).
  • To send account-related messages (e.g., password reset) and optionally user support replies.
  • To analyze and improve app performance and reliability.

Data Storage & Security

We use Supabase (Postgres) to store user data. We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information, but no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong password and keep your device secure.

Third Parties

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising. We may use third-party services (hosting, analytics) that process data on our behalf — those providers are contractually required to protect your information.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete your profile and tasks from inside CoTick. Deleting your account will remove your personal data from our active systems (some backups or logs may persist for a limited time).
  • If you want a full data deletion or export, contact us using the email below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

CoTick is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have such data, please contact us.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want to request data access/deletion, email: dnadigitalstudios@gmail.com.
