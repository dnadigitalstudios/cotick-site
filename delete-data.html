Delete your data in CoTick

CoTick allows you to delete specific data such as individual tasks, teams, or lists, without deleting your entire account.

Steps to delete data

  1. Open the CoTick app.
  2. Select the task or team you wish to delete.
  3. Use the Delete or Remove option in the app menu.
  4. Deleted items are permanently removed from your account and cannot be restored.

Need help deleting data?

If you’d like us to delete data for you (without deleting your account), email dnadigitalstudios@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my CoTick data" and include your registered email or phone number.

What data is deleted

  • All tasks, lists, or team data that you specify.
  • Associated comments or notes inside those tasks.

What data we retain

Basic account information (name, email, phone number) remains active until you request full account deletion.

Need full deletion instead? Visit Delete Account.
