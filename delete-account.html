Delete your CoTick account

We’re sorry to see you go. If you would like to delete your CoTick account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to request deletion

  1. Send an email to dnadigitalstudios@gmail.com using the subject line "Delete my CoTick account".
  2. Include your registered email address or phone number so we can identify your account.
  3. We will delete your account and associated data (teams, tasks, membership) within 7 business days.

What data is deleted

  • All task lists and team memberships you created.
  • Your profile information (name, phone, and email).
  • All other user-generated content.

What data we may retain

For security and legal compliance, basic server logs and backup copies may be retained for up to 30 days before being automatically purged.

If you reinstall CoTick later, you will be able to create a new account from scratch.

Need help? Contact dnadigitalstudios@gmail.com.
